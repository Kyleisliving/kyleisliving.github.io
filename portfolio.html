Summaware
Lead UI/UX Designer | Summaware.com

Bringing one of the oldest industries into the digital age. Summaware is a multi-platform management software covering all aspects it takes to produce and distribute news publications.

As the Lead UI/UX designer, my vision for the project has been to create a streamline & easy to use interface that makes employees excited to return to the system everyday. Utilize vibrant/warm colors to make the mundane tasks welcoming and easy on the eyes. I've also been spearheading the process of technical writing/documentation by creating how-to guides for the employees who use Summaware daily to assist in their regular workflows and streamline their tasks.

Bangor Daily News
UI/UX Designer

Bangor Daily News was a client that's a newspaper publication distributing news to a large portion of central and eastern Maine. They requested designs for an updated workflow for their subcription purchase process. That's where I come in.

My role as UI/UX Designer was collaborating with their design team to create a streamline and mordern flow utilizing guidelines that were requested. One of the key design focuses I made was showing that their customer's are contributors to the legacy of this century old newspaper. That they are apart of the team that makes up the beautiful history of Bangor Daily News.

Criterion Channel
UI/UX Design

Criteiron Channel is a streaming service that specializes in preservation and archiving of film. This app allows users to experience a wide range of films from all across the globe along with providing ample amounts of bonus features.

This was just a "for fun" project I did for myself as I am an avid fan and love using the app. I just wanted to dream how I could improve the app's search functionality and make a more refined and fun user experience. Again, I have never worked with Criterion Channel and this was just an evening project I did to challenge myself with mobile design.
